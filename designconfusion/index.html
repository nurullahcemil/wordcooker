Our Teaching Metod

Try Fail Fix

Learn by failure

When you encounter failure, tackle it head on and learn from your mistakes. Realize that every idea that pops up in your mind isn't going to work. Take the time to organize your thoughts after a failure and realize what you did wrong. Above all else, be willing to learn and grow.

Who is the Instructor?

Hello this is Nurullah, I have been working as a designer for more than 13 years now. I have 4 applications are on the app store with over 500.000 users. I worked on all kind of design projects in the past. I am living in Hawaii. I am a traveller besides designing. This is the best part of being a designer. It gives you freedom to work anywhere.

I took tons of classes on various topics and I had chance to analyze every single teachersâ€™ method. I realized that the most effective classes has one thing in common, they get your hands dirty at first.

I learned UX and UI in a hard way. It took me years to understand the concept. But luckly I had chance to fail so many times. I believe if we teach technical terminology and details at first, we make it so hard to understand. I decided to reverse the teaching method. Now I can even teach it to kids.
